Vocabulary for the Representation of the Commercial Public Debt of a City Council: Description back to index

Figure 1 shows the classes and properties of the vocabulary that represents the commercial public debt of a city council:

In this vocabulary there are casses to represent the late payment quarterly report [esdeucom:InformeMorosidadTrimestral](#InformeMorosidadTrimestral) and the late payment monthly report [esdeucom:InformePMPMensual](#InformePMPMensual). The properties of these two clases correspond to the fields of each of these reports. Some of these fields are the monthly average payment period [esdeucom:periodoMedioPagoMensual](#periodoMedioPagoMensual) or monthly PMP and the quarterly average payment period [esdeucom:periodoMedioPagoTrimestral](#periodoMedioPagoTrimestral) or quarterly PMP. The monthly and quarterly late payment reports cover a certain period [esdeucom:periodo](#periodo) in a way that is possible to represent the late payment reports of several months or quarters and thus, be able to make comparative analysis on the commercial debt of a city council over time. Besides this, the monthly PMP is computed globally for the city council and is represented through the property [esdeucom:periodoMedioPagoMensualGlobal](#periodoMedioPagoMensualGlobal) in the class [esdeucom:InformePMPMensualGlobal](#InformePMPMensualGlobal). In order to represent the period we have reused the [time](https://www.w3.org/TR/owl-time/) ontology, specifically the classes s[time:ProperInterval](#http://www.w3.org/2006/time#ProperInterval) and [time:Instant](#http://www.w3.org/2006/time#Instant) to represent the beginning and end of a period. The late payment reports are made for the local entity as well as for its depending entities. The local entity and its dependent entities are represented with the class [org:Organization](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-org/#class-organization) from the [Organization](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-org/) ontology.

3.1 Examples back to index

In order to facilitate the comprehension of the use of some classes and properties of this vocabulary, some examples will be provided below. The first example refers to the quaterly late payment report of a dependent entity of the Madrid City Council, specifically we show the report for the Madrid Municipal Transport Agency at the end of the fourth quarter, 2019. 
<http://vocab.ciudadesabiertas.es/recurso/economia/deuda-comercial/informe-morosidad-trimestral/2019-cuarto-12-28-079-AP-001>

        esdeucom:entidad  <http://vocab.ciudadesabiertas.es/recurso/economia/deuda-comercial/organizacion/12-28-079-AP-001>;
        esdeucom:periodo <http://vocab.ciudadesabiertas.es/recurso/economia/deuda-comercial/intervalo-tiempo/2019-cuarto-trimestre>;
        esdeucom:periodoMedioPagoTrimestral "55.50"^^xsd:float ;
        esdeucom:numPagosDentroPeriodo "6472"^^xsd:integer ;
        esdeucom:importePagosDentroPeriodo "51831754.13"^^xsd:float ;
        esdeucom:numPagosFueraPeriodo "1124"^^xsd:integer ;
        esdeucom:importePagosFueraPeriodo "5579630.33"^^xsd:float ;
        esdeucom:numPagosInteresesDemora "0"^^xsd:integer ;
        esdeucom:importePagosInteresesDemora "0.0"^^xsd:float ;
        esdeucom:periodoMedioPagoPendiente "33.87"^^xsd:float ;
        esdeucom:numFacturasPendientesDentroPeriodo "3020"^^xsd:integer ;
        esdeucom:importeFacturasPendientesDentroPeriodo "34441734.41"^^xsd:float ;
        esdeucom:numFacturasPendientesFueraPeriodo "25"^^xsd:integer ;
        esdeucom:importeFacturasPendientesFueraPeriodo "489769.85"^^xsd:float ;
